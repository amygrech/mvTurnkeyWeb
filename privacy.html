We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information. Data submitted through the contact form is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel who are required to maintain confidentiality.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiries and to comply with legal or regulatory requirements. Messages sent through the contact form are stored securely and deleted periodically to ensure data minimization.

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:
